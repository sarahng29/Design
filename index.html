Game Boy (original)


Game Boy (card version)

I decided to use the "Game Boy" look to make cards. On the first page, the screen of "Game Boy" will be cut, while the second to fourth pages are used to compose messages. Some photos will be pasted on pages 2 to 4 at the top of the center. Therefore, we can see the photo of the second page on the first page. For the last page, a photo will also be pasted at the bottom, and the photo will be covered by paper with a cross logo.
